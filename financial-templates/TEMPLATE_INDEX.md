## 🔍 Template Details

### Core Templates
- **General Ledger**: Transaction ID, Date, Account, Description, Debit, Credit, Balance
- **Balance Sheet**: Assets (Current/Non-Current), Liabilities, Equity
- **Income Statement**: Revenue, COGS, Gross Profit, Operating Expenses, Net Income
- **Cash Flow**: Operating, Investing, Financing activities
- **Annual Budget**: 12-month grid with quarterly totals

### Analysis Templates
- **Financial Ratios**: Liquidity, Profitability, Efficiency, Solvency ratios
- **Variance Analysis**: Budget vs Actual with variance %
- **Break-Even**: Fixed costs, variable costs, break-even point

### Management Templates
- **Accounts Payable**: Invoice tracking, due dates, payment status
- **Accounts Receivable**: Customer invoices, aging, collection status
- **Payroll**: Employee data, gross pay, deductions, net pay
- **Inventory**: SKU, quantity, reorder level, total value
- **Expense Tracker**: Date, category, description, amount, reimbursement

### Advanced Templates
- **Depreciation**: Asset tracking, multiple depreciation methods
- **Loan Amortization**: Payment schedule, principal/interest breakdown
- **Chart of Accounts**: Complete account structure
- **Monthly Report**: KPI dashboard with comparisons
- **Tax Worksheet**: Income, deductions, tax calculation
- **Capital Budgeting**: NPV, IRR, payback, profitability index
- **Working Capital**: Current assets/liabilities, cash conversion cycle

### Industry Templates
- **Retail P&L**: Sales, COGS, gross margin, operating expenses
- **Service P&L**: Revenue streams, direct costs, gross profit
- **Restaurant P&L**: Food/beverage sales, prime cost, labor cost
- **Startup Financials**: 12-month projections, burn rate, runway
- **Real Estate**: Purchase price, financing, rental income, ROI
